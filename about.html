Our Story: Two Generations of Pure Dairy

We didn't start PeakPureCow Milk to build a brand. We started it because we believe every family deserves dairy they can trust.

It Started With a Single Cow

In 2025, our grandparents bought a small plot of land in Attock, Pakistan with one jersey cow and a dream. They didn't have a business plan — just a belief that honest farming produces the best food.

That single cow became a herd. That small plot became 200 acres of lush, pesticide-free pastureland. And that belief? It became the foundation of everything we do today.

Happy Cows, Honest Milk

Our cows aren't numbers — they're family. They graze freely on open pastures, eat a natural grass-fed diet, and are never treated with growth hormones or unnecessary antibiotics.

We believe the way you treat your animals shows up in every glass of milk. That's why we invest in their wellbeing first, and let the quality speak for itself.

From Pasture to Bottle in 48 Hours

Every morning begins at dawn. Our farmers hand-select the freshest milk, which is gently pasteurized and bottled on-site — never shipped to third-party processors.

From the moment it leaves our cows to the moment it arrives at your door, every step is handled by our team. That's traceability you won't find on a supermarket shelf.
