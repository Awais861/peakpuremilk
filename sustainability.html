Good Dairy Starts With a Healthy Planet

We believe the best food comes from farms that give back more than they take. Here's how we're building a dairy industry the Earth can be proud of.

Regenerative Grazing

We practice managed rotational grazing, moving our herds across pastures in a pattern that lets the land rest and regenerate. The result? Healthier soil that absorbs more carbon, richer grass, and cows that produce better milk.

Independent soil tests show our pastures sequester 3.2 tons of carbon per acre annually — turning our farm into a carbon sink, not a source.

Zero-Waste Packaging

Every PeakPureCow Milk product ships in packaging designed to leave no trace. Our milk comes in returnable glass bottles. Our butter is wrapped in compostable parchment.

For deliveries, we use insulated boxes made from recycled cardboard and plant-based ice packs that melt into water and plant food. Toss them in your garden — seriously.

Animal Welfare First

Our cows live the way cows should — outdoors, in herds, with room to roam. We exceed every national animal welfare standard and submit to annual third-party audits to prove it.

  • Year-round pasture access (weather permitting)
  • No growth hormones or routine antibiotics
  • Veterinarian on staff for preventive care
  • Certified Humane® and Animal Welfare Approved
